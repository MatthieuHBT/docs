---
title: Comptes publicitaires
api: GET https://roapix.up.railway.app/api/v1/accounts
description: Liste les comptes Meta Ads connectés à votre workspace.
---

<ParamField header="Authorization" type="string" required>
  `Bearer roapix_votre_clé`
</ParamField>

## Réponse

<ResponseField name="data" type="array">
  Liste des comptes publicitaires uniques.
  <Expandable title="Propriétés de chaque item">
    <ResponseField name="account_id" type="string">ID Meta du compte (ex: `act_123456`)</ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="account_name" type="string">Nom affiché du compte</ResponseField>
  </Expandable>
</ResponseField>

```bash cURL
  -H "Authorization: Bearer roapix_votre_clé"
```
